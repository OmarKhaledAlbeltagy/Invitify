About The Conference

During the TEOXANE Academy Saudia you will have the opportunity to meet TEOXANE experts and to discover the latest innovations and techniques in Hyaluronic Acid treatments.

Our experts (international and local) will be there to share their experience on different topics and indications.

There will be a packed itinerary of scientific and anatomical lectures and live demonstrations, covering areas including the tear trough, lip anatomy, sculpting of the face and contouring of the mid-face.
